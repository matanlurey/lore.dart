Level

Level class final

Controls the verbosity of logging output by a logger.

Levels are ordered by their severity, with Level.debug being the least severe and Level.fatal being the most severe. The default level is typically Level.status; programs can interpret a level provided as a configuration string by using Level.tryParse or Level.parse.
