Future<void> flush()

Flushes any buffered data to the sink.

This method is not required to be implemented by all sinks, and may be a no-op if the sink does not buffer data, but is a best practice to call this method before closing the underlying resource or terminating the program.

The future completes when the data has been successfully written.
