Creates a new RawLogger that logs messages to LogSink.

Custom formatting can be provided by setting formatString and formatBytes respectively.

In addition:

  • If level is provided, only messages of an equal or higher level are logged.

  • If throwOnFatal is provided, it is used instead of throwing FatalError.
